About Me


Hi. I'm Travis I am an illustrator, designer and constant student of the arts based out of Philadelphia, Pa. I love art in all its forms and especially enjoy the challenge of telling a story with one still image. I'm a singer, runner and over-all huge nerd. Good acappella music is the quickest way to my heart. I get jazzed about witty screenprint t's, clean logos and illustration with character and these are some of my favorite types of projects. So check out some of my work and get in touch with me. I can't wait to work with you on your next project!
